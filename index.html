Birman Cat

The Birman, also known as the Sacred Cat of Burma, is strikingly beautiful with a cream coloured coat, distinctive points and bright blue eyes.

  • Breed Name: Birman
  • Size: Medium to Large
  • Weight: 9-12 lbs
  • Coat: Medium to Long
  • Lifespan: 12 to 16 years
  • Habitat: Indoors: curled up on their owner's bed, asleep on their lap or inside any box, drawer or laundry basket they can find!

Birman cats have beautiful long hair, a medium to heavy body weight, brilliant blue eyes and a silky coat in a variety of different coloured points including blue, seal, chocolate, lilac, red, cream, tabby and tortie. Birmans have four white gloves on each of their feet.

The Birman cat is intelligent, affectionate, gentle, and tolerant. They are very playful and the breed is very hardy and healthy. They are a placid cat and very loyal to their owners. They are also well suited to families with children.
